About Us

We are a team of two passionate computer science students in our second year of university. Our goal is to create an innovative platform for learning music while listening to favorite tunes. We believe in combining technology and creativity to offer a unique learning experience.

Our Story

Cyrine Hammad and William Hammad are university students who share a passion for music and technology. Our project was born from the idea that learning music should be enjoyable and accessible, combining education and entertainment.
