Guitar

The guitar is a versatile stringed instrument used in various genres like rock, pop, jazz, and classical music.

  • Acoustic and electric versions
  • 6 to 12 strings
  • Wide range of playing styles

Piano

The piano is a classic keyboard instrument with a rich sound, used in classical, jazz, and contemporary music.

  • 88 keys
  • Grand and upright models
  • Common in many music genres

Violin

The violin is a stringed instrument used in classical, folk, and other genres.

  • Four strings
  • Requires a bow to play
  • Known for its expressive sound

Drums

Drums are percussion instruments used in various genres like rock, jazz, and marching bands.

  • Different drum types
  • Integral part of rhythm sections
  • Used with other percussion instruments
